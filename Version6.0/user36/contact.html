The World Cup is the most exciting competition since it only happens every 4 years with only international teams! This is where players get noticed for bigger clubs and where peoples culture pop out a lot!

𝑻𝒉𝒆 𝑾𝒐𝒓𝒍𝒅 𝑪𝒖𝒑: 𝑨 𝑩𝒓𝒊𝒆𝒇 𝑶𝒗𝒆𝒓𝒗𝒊𝒆𝒘

There are 45 teams that are qualified for the World Cup, international teams can only qualifty if they do well in the other competitions for example the Euros and the Copa America that happens 2 years previous. Once teams qualify they then get drawn into group stages, once that is done each team will play at least 4 games no matter what. The top 2 teams from each bracket will advance to the round of 16 and the rest will be elimnated, then this is where the knockouts start to happen. After the round of 16 it then becomes the quarter finals, semi finals anf finals. To determine the World Cup Champion!!!
